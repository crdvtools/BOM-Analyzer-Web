âœ… It matches the license used by the original BOM Analyzer desktop app

âœ… It allows anyone (individuals, companies, other teams) to freely use,

âœ… It has no "copyleft" requirement â€” teams can use this in commercial settings

âœ… Simple and clear â€” only 3 paragraphs, recognized worldwide

AGPL-3.0 was considered but rejected because it would require any organization

friction for PCB teams who may want to customize without open-sourcing.

GPL was similarly rejected for derivative commercial use concerns.

MIT is the right choice for a community tool in this domain.
